THERE IS A LOT OF OBSOLETE HIGH END HI-FI, COLLECTING DUST.

Bang & Olufsen has focused on branding their Beoplay series. The product line is more compatible with iOS, Android, and it is also more affordable for the common smartphone user.

B&O has released great products since the 70s and their designs are considered to be timeless. Unfortunately, the technology of these timeless pieces are obsolete and now collecting dust.

With the ”OldbutNew” campaign, you can turn in your Beosystem, get it refurbished, stream with Bluetooth and be able to log in with Spotify. Then, you can listen to the songs of the artists you still haven’t found in the record stores.

To promote this, an ad is published on one of many online classified advertisings sites such as Craigslist, eBay and Blocket. Swedens biggest advertising site. Then the ad can be spread through tech blogs and sites which will build up the hype. When you contact the seller by mail or phone, a CTA forwards you to the campaign site where you can buy the a fully furbished Beosystem or the nearest location if you already own a system that you want to refurbish.

Through the social media channels such as Instagram and Facebook using the hashtag “#OldbutNew” you can get in touch with the B&O certified store to get your Beosystem modernized.
