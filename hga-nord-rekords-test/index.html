HOW CAN HNR OVEREXPOSE AS A BRAND, WITHOUT GETTING ”MAINSTREAM”?

Höga Nord Rekords is a record label based in Gothenburg, Sweden with signed bands such as Uran GBG, Fontän, Exorcist GBG and more.

Their music is typically suited for driving.* Todays radio stations play music that don’t fit in with the driving experience.

By launching a pirate internet station Höga Nord Radio targets the group drivers automatically to the frequency as a emergency interuption for a brief moment. This exposure will get the driver to be eager on what music is played. *
