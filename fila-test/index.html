HOW CAN FILA BE A HIGH STATUS BRAND again?

One of the first clothing brand for athletes established in 1911.

FILA was the first choice for Playboys and in the 80s, it became a significant importance to the british subculture “The Casuals”, who roamed the football stadiums and pubs.

Today, the brand has targeted to be street wear and lacks the exclusivity it once had during the time when a famous brand ambassador wore an outfit, winning tournaments and was spotted in both sports and fashion magazines.

Using existing pictures historical and pop cultural referenced of celebrities wearing FILA. Every print has a content copy about the moment the picture was taken. The copy can be about Björn Borgs victories, George Michael’s time in Wham and Tupacs leisure moments.

With the tagline “The year was…”, the recipient will get the sense of a lesson in pop culture history.
