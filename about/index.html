That’s me! Dressed like Björn Borg and groomed like Freddie Mercury.

 

A Creative copywriter, Shaped in Sweden.

Telling crazy-funny stories for friends and family gave me the validation that I’m good at ”story telling.” It started from a very young age hearing my parents stories at the dinner table.

My fascination for music got me building my own electric guitar. The art of film got me searching for ”hard to get” B-flicks. Learning the principles of design made me understand the importance of functionality.

It only took one conversation with a dear friend of mine who works as a creative art director at a PR-agency to take the leap of faith I applied to be the copywriter program at Forsbergs School in Stockholm.

My USP is like my personality. Advertising ought to be funny, crazy and different. Funny is communicative, crazy is aesthetic and different is research.
